Echo

Location: World Map (outside of Daila)

Echo will join automatically.

Fog

Location: Temple

Fog can be found after obtaining the Lash psynergy and returning into the dungeon.

Breath

Location: Shrine of the Sea God

Breath can be found after obtaining the Lash psynergy and descending as deep into the dungeon as can be reached.

Cannon

Location: Plateau

Cannon can be found after obtaining the Pound psynergy and solving a Pound puzzle.

Zagan

Location: Temple

Zagan can be found in a cave to the west of the Dehkan Plateau and solving a Move puzzle.
